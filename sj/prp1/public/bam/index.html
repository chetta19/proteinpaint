Download the DNAnexus FileViewer

DNAnexus provides a secure way to view unpublished/sensitive BAM files as custom ProteinPaint BAM tracks. In doing so, randomized URLs are produced pointing to your files hosted on the DNAnexus platform, expiring in 24 hours. Through the FileViewer mechanism, ProteinPaint accesses these URLs and launches the track. Only the index portions of your BAM files will be downloaded to the ProteinPaint server, but not the BAM files. The index files do not contain actual sequencing reads.

Refer to procedures in this document, to obtain the ProteinPaint BAM FileViewer and run it in your DNAnexus project.
